About the Event

🇮🇳 Youth as Game Changers for Viksit Bharat

EvolVIT Club proudly organized an inspiring orientation session on “Youth as Game Changers for Viksit Bharat”, featuring a distinguished Brigadier as the keynote speaker.

The session emphasized the pivotal role of youth in shaping the future of India through innovation, leadership, discipline, and a strong commitment to nation-building.

Students gained valuable insights into how their ideas and actions can contribute to the vision of Viksit Bharat 2047 and create a meaningful impact on society.
