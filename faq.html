❓ Frequently Asked Questions

👥 Who can join EvolVIT Club?
Any student with an interest in technology, innovation, design, or research can join the club.
📝 How can I join the club?
Registrations open during recruitment phases. Keep an eye on announcements and updates from the club.
🚀 What kind of events does EvolVIT organize?
We organize hackathons, ideathons, workshops, industrial visits, and creative competitions.
💻 Do I need technical knowledge to join?
Not necessarily. Beginners are always welcome! We provide opportunities to learn and grow.
🏆 What are the benefits of joining?
You gain practical experience, teamwork skills, exposure to real-world projects, and networking opportunities.
📅 How often are events conducted?
Events are conducted regularly throughout the semester depending on schedules and collaborations.
